How to play?

  1. Choose a language and press Start (required by browser audio policy).
  2. Play by clicking pads or keys: W=Crash, A=Kick, S=Snare, D=Tom-1, J=Tom-2, K=Tom-3, L=Tom-4.
  3. Open Customize to remap keys, change theme colors, or rearrange pads.
  4. Turn on Free layout to drag pads anywhere on stage. Turn off to grid & drag-reorder.
  5. Upload your own background image for Welcome or App screen; choose fit and overlay.
  6. Press Exit to return here; all sounds stop.

Pro tips

  • Hold a key for fast rolls; combine with clicks for fills.
  • High-contrast key badges help on bright images.
  • Your setup is saved locally (no network).
